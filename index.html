Influx and outflow of the Evangelical Lutheran Church of Finland

In this study we look into if and how the Lutheran Church position on current news and topical issues have influenced the numbers of people who resigned / joined the Church; if there is a correlation between public discussion and changes in legislation and number of members of the Church.
